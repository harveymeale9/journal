Plate I — The aperture turns; the centre holds.
(Plates in the living edition move.)

Attention behaves like the aperture drawn above: what widens admits more world and less detail; what narrows admits detail and loses the world. Neither setting is correct. The error is forgetting that there is a setting at all. See also: Entry V, The Economy of Meaning.
