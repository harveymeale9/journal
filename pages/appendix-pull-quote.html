Appendix · The Quotation Form

✦
❝ The map is a rumour;
the territory is a visit.

Entry IV · Maps Are Not Territory

❦

A saying worth carrying is set apart in this manner, ruled above and below in brass, with its source given in the small capitals of the Manual. Around it, ordinary matter resumes: the page holds one raised voice at a time, and the quiet text beneath it carries on as though nothing had been said.
