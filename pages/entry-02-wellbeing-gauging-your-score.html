Entry II · Well-Being

A simple way of gauging your own EWB would be to consider your answer if I simply asked you, how's life?

If you responded with, "Uhh, kind of shitty lately, to be honest," then it means you are likely experiencing more unwanted emotions than desirable ones. You likely have a low emotional well-being.

If you'd responded with, "Absolutely, incredible, couldn't be better!," then you're saying your life feels good, in which case you have a high EWB, congrats.

The subconscious mind treats emotional well-being like a computer game.

Every time you experience a positive emotion, its score increases by some amount, and every time you experience a negative emotion, its score decreases.
