Specimen · Terms of Art

A Brief Lexicon

❦

Aperture
The width of attention at a given hour; what it admits and what it refuses.
Entry
A single idea stated once, completely, and left alone.
Gutter
The valley between two pages; also, the part of a thought that binds it to its neighbour.
Keyholder
One for whom the sealed leaves open.
Plate
A drawn figure; in the living edition, one that may move.
Rubric
Matter set in oxblood: laws, numerals, and the first letters of things.
Sealed
Written, but not yet yours.
