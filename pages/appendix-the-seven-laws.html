The Seven Laws

✦ ✦ ✦

  1. Attention. Whatever you attend to, you enlarge.
  2. Reflection. Every judgment describes the judge before the judged.
  3. Cartography. The map is a rumour; the territory is a visit.
  4. Economy. Meaning is spent wherever attention is paid.
  5. Thresholds. Nothing changes except at a door.
  6. Return. What is refused arrives again, wearing new clothes.
  7. Silence. The Manual ends where the reader begins.
