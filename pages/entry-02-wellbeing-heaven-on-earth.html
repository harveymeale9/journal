Entry II · Well-Being

The highest possible emotional well-being feels like what I refer to as Heaven on Earth.

Heaven on Earth is the experience of only the most intensely positive emotions. There are no negative emotions in Heaven. It's the strongest conceivable expression of pure bliss, love, joy, excitement, etc.

Now, you must understand, Heaven on Earth doesn't have a particular location anywhere in the physical world. Your Heaven on Earth could look incredibly different to mine. But wherever your Heaven on Earth is, whatever you're doing, whomever you're with, Heaven on Earth will always feel like perfection.

Heaven has no address; yours need not resemble mine. Both feel like perfection.
