Entry II · Well-Being

Although it's not possible, let's imagine being able to quantifiably measure the total emotional well-being of someone's life to determine, objectively, how enjoyable their life had been.

I want you to imagine that there was a device that could take a reading of your emotional state every second of the day. This device would determine exactly what emotion you were experiencing, as well as the intensity of that emotion, and would correspondingly spit out an "emotional well-being score" for every second of every day, for your entire life.

Suppose the most intensely positive emotion would give you a +100 emotional quality score (this would mean you're in Heaven on Earth), and the most intensely negative emotion would be -100 (Hell on Earth).
