On Emotional
Well-Being

✦ ✦ ✦

Emotional well-being is essentially a measure of the quality of one's emotional states. In other words, it's a measure of what it feels like to be you.

Specifically, emotional well-being is measured by the ratio of positive emotional states relative to negative emotional states over a given duration.

Plate — The wager of emotions: what is felt for, against what is felt against, over time.
