Specimen · A Working

The Evening
Recension

✦ ✦ ✦

  1. At the day's end, name aloud the three things that held your attention longest. Do not judge them yet.
  2. Ask of each: did I choose this, or did it choose me? Mark the answer in the margin of the day.
  3. Choose one object of attention for tomorrow, and one refusal. A refusal unnamed is a door left open.
  4. Close the book. The practice ends with the closing, not before.
