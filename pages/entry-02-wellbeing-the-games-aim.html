The Reality Manual

The aim of the game is simple: get the highest possible score before the fat lady sings!

Remember, this is not necessarily the game you're playing (as the conscious mind), but this is how your subconscious mind is interfacing with reality.

You (conscious mind) might be concerned with personal growth, being a good/ethical person, achieving material success, etc. But your subconscious has no idea what any of that means. All it's thinking about is, "How do I make my experience of reality feel better?"

So if the subconscious' objective in life is to maximize your emotional well-being, what would a highly successful life feel like?
