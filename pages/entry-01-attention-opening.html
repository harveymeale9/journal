The Reality Manual

Entry the First

On the Nature
of Attention

✦ ✦ ✦

Whatever you attend to, you enlarge. This is the first law of the Manual, and every later entry is a footnote to it. The world does not arrive whole; it arrives selected, and the instrument of selection is attention — which means the instrument is you.

Most struggle is therefore a dispute about aim, not about the world. The anxious mind is not perceiving more danger; it is aiming at danger with great skill.
