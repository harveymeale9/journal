Specimen · The Lecturer's Slate

A Law, Written
as It Is Spoken

Plate V — The lecturer's slate: a law written as it is spoken,
then wiped clean to be written again.

Where a law wants the feel of a lecture — chalk, slate, the hand mid-thought — it is set upon the slate, and the page writes it in real time. See Law IV, The Economy of Meaning.
